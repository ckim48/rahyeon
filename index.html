About the Project

FitStride 2.0 — AR Running Meets Chemistry

FitStride 2.0 enhances a traditional running app by integrating metaverse and AR technology, turning exercise into a dynamic educational experience.

As users run along real-world routes, they collect AR-rendered atoms at GPS-based checkpoints. Over multiple runs, these atoms can be combined to form molecules, helping students learn molecular structures in a fun and interactive way while staying active.

Why We Made This Project

Our Purpose

Many students struggle to stay motivated in both fitness and academics. FitStride 2.0 bridges these worlds by combining exercise with hands-on learning.

By encouraging physical activity through running challenges and reinforcing chemistry knowledge with interactive AR molecules, our goal is to promote health, engagement, and education in a single, gamified experience.
